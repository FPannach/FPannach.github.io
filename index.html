About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in Information Science and Digital Humanities at the Centre for Language and Cognition (CLCG) of the Rijksuniversiteit Groningen (Netherlands). My research is focussed on Computational Folkloristics and Digital Mythological Studies. To find out which stories people tell and how they tell them, I use NLP and Semantic Web techniques to model and analyze narratives. I am passionate about teaching and supervision, and strongly support the Open Science. I hold a Doctorate Degree in Computer Science from the University of Göttingen, Germany.
